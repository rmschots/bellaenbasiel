Culture

With its historical buildings and imposing city gates Bruges belongs to Unesco World Heritage site. Numerous museums and exhibitions fill the culture finder.

Sports and nature

Around the city there are important green footpaths and nature reserves. And there is also a possibility to safely ride your bike on the cycle lanes around the city.
You can also reach the coast (e.g. the nature reserve ‘het Zwin’ and the polders near Uitkerke) by bike, while visiting colourful polder villages.
The Dutch border town of Sluis is only 20 kilometres away and the book village of Damme is even within walking distance.
