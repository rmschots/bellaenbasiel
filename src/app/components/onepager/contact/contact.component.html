Access

check By train: b-rail.be
check By bus: In front of the station take bus nr 4, 14, or 43 and ask the driver to stop at the stop Carmersbrug. From 20.00 o’clock in the evening, you have to take the evening line koolkerke-Dudzele nr 90. You walk over the Carmersbridge and you find our house in the 3th street on the left. This is only 200 m to walk.
check By taxi: ca 10€
check By car: Loading and unloading is allowed in front of our house. We’ll help you to find parking space.
