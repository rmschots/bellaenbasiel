Our house

The guests have their own room and bathroom. Breakfast is served in the dining room or outside in our sunny and flowered garden.
Our chickens don’t like other pets.

Our neighborhood

We gladly welcome you in our guestroom, situated in the quiet and green Saint Ann district, a working-class area within the old city walls of Bruges.
Via a 10-minute walk along picturesque small streets you will arrive at the city centre, from where you can enjoy numerous historic buildings, events, museums, festivals, exhibitions…
